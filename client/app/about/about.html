Word from our Founder:


As an avid fisherman my entire life, I remember going on party boats in New Jersey and other vacation spots since I was a young child. It has always been a special time for me since it was when I bonded the most with friends and family, from my father and Mother to grandfather and niece. It had been a few years since I had been on one so I decided to search the internet to find one near me, to my surprise it took me 15 minutes to finally find one, and another hour to find others. I thought this was a huge problem! I believe now a days, nothing should take more than two minutes by searching the web. So I decided to set out on building a site that would stream line the searching process. Now with findpartyboats.com up and running, anyone can find a party boat anywhere in the United States as long as there is one nearby. There are two ways to search. One can search by a zip code, or if they are unsure of the zip code, by state. I hope through this site, more people will go on charter boats and revive this once thriving industry and make unforgettable memories with their family and friends.
